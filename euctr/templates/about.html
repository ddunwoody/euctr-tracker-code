What's this all about?

Non-publication of clinical trial results is an ongoing global public health problem. The European Union (EU) recently enacted legislation requiring all trials on the European Union Clinical Trials Register (EUCTR) to post results to the registry within 12 months of completion. Because the US FDA Amendment Act 2007 has not yet been formally implemented, the EU law is the first legislation on trial publication to be enacted anywhere in the world. The enforcement date of this legislation was December 21st 2016. This site shows compliance rates, and identifies features associated with non-compliance.
